Common questions

GTM shows ✓ Found but GA4 / Google Ads shows ✗ Missing — what does that mean?

GTM is installed, but the tags inside it aren't firing correctly on this page. Check your trigger conditions in GTM — they may not be matching this URL.

Google Ads shows "Tag present in GTM — no ping seen yet" — is that a problem?

The tag exists in your GTM container but didn't fire when this page loaded. This usually means the trigger isn't set up correctly, or you're previewing the page in GTM debug mode which blocks some tags.

GA4 shows "Tag loaded — no hit captured yet"

GA4's script is on the page but no measurement hit was recorded in the network. This can happen with ad blockers, or if the GA4 Configuration tag has a trigger other than "All Pages".

Does this work on password-protected pages?

Yes! Unlike URL-based checkers, this bookmarklet runs directly in your browser, so it works on any page you can view — including pages behind a login.

Does it send any of my data anywhere?

No. The bookmarklet runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to Teach Traffic's servers — it's all local JavaScript reading your own page.

I can't drag the button — how do I install it manually?

Right-click your bookmarks bar and choose "Add bookmark" or "Add page". Paste the bookmarklet URL (which starts with javascript:) into the URL field and name it "Tracking Checker".
